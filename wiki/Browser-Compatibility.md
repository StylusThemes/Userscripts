## Userscript Manager Details

### Popular Managers

- **Tampermonkey**: Feature-rich, widely supported, good for beginners
- **Violentmonkey**: Open-source alternative, lightweight and privacy-focused
- **ScriptCat**: Modern manager with good development features

### Browser-Specific Notes

- **Safari**: Requires dedicated apps from the App Store
- **QQ Browser**: Comes with Tampermonkey and Violentmonkey pre-installed
- **Mobile Browsers**: May have limited extension support, check device compatibility

## Troubleshooting

If you're having issues:

1. Ensure your userscript manager is up to date
2. Check that the script is enabled in your manager
3. Verify the `@match` patterns in the script headers
4. Clear browser cache and reload

For more help, check the [main repository](https://github.com/StylusThemes/Userscripts) or open an issue.
